About Me

Howdy!!! My name is Muhammad Nauman and I am a Computer Science graduate from Texas A&M University with a minor in Cyber Security. I started attending Texas A&M University on August, 2018, and graduated May, 2021! I graduated as an Undergraduate Research Scholar with Engineering Honors and Magna Cum Laude designation. I have interned at WellMed Medical Management, which is a child company of United Health Group. I was an Application Developer Intern for the Business PMO Team at WellMed, San Antonio.

I went to high school at Newman Smith High School. I was part of the Chess Club and the UIL Club. I was the president of Mathematics UIL Team and the Computer Science UIL Team. I loved number sense in which you had to solve as many questions as you can correctly in a limited time. I was a Honor Graduate of class of 2018.

A little bit more about me. I was born in Karachi, Pakistan. I have 1 younger sister. I love going to Texas A&M University. I love helping others. My hobbies include chess, problem-solving, playing video games, cooking (& eating), reading, and learning new things. My favorite colors are green and white. An interesting fact about me is that I have ridden on an elephant and on a camel (on separate occasions). I can read, write, and speak English and Urdu. I can also speak and understand Hindi!
